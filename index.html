Ed Sheeran is an English singer-songwriter. Inspired by personal events and experiences, Sheeran writes and sings pop-ballad songs, many of which have broken records in various countries, such as the U.S., Germany and Australia, for top positions in national charts. Unlike many mainstream artists, Sheeran does not only sing about broken hearts and lost love; rather, he delves deeper into the meaning of family and growing up. With a smooth, soulful voice and lyrics that reflect the depth of his feelings, any listener would be sure to fall in love with his music style and personality.
